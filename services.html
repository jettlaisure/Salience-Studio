COMMON QUESTIONS

How much does a website cost?

Web design at Salience Studio starts from $1,000 for a custom-built brand site. Final price depends on scope — page count, features, e-commerce requirements, and timeline. Every project is quoted individually after a discovery call.

How long does a website build take?

Most brand sites take 3–6 weeks from kickoff to launch. Timeline depends on scope, content readiness, and how quickly feedback rounds move. E-commerce builds typically take longer — 6–10 weeks.

What is AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation)?

AEO is the practice of optimising content to be cited by AI tools like Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and Perplexity. It involves structuring content so AI systems can extract and attribute it as a direct answer to a user's question — placing the brand in AI-generated results pages alongside or above traditional search.

How long does SEO take to show results?

SEO typically shows meaningful results in 3–6 months. Technical fixes and on-page changes can improve rankings within weeks. Content and authority-building takes longer — most clients see significant organic growth between months 4 and 9.

What is digital PR and how does it help my brand?

Digital PR secures editorial coverage and influencer placements in online publications and social channels. It builds brand authority, generates high-quality backlinks that strengthen SEO, and puts the brand in front of audiences who trust the publication covering it.

How does custom pricing work?

Every engagement is scoped and priced individually based on your brand's specific needs and goals. We provide a custom proposal within 48 hours of the discovery call, with a clear breakdown of deliverables and cost. No surprise fees after kickoff.

Do you work on retainer or project basis?

Both. Web design is project-based with a fixed fee. The Organic Online Marketing package runs as a monthly retainer — because organic visibility compounds over time and results build with consistency.

What's the minimum engagement size?

There's no fixed minimum, but we work best with brands that are serious about growth and have a budget aligned with their ambitions. Book a call and we'll be upfront about fit — no pressure either way.
